Helpful Advice

💡 Reporting a Lost Item Be as specific as possible — include a unique description (scratches, stickers, serial number), the exact location where you last had it, and the approximate time. The more detail, the easier it is to match.
✅ What To Do When You Find Something Hand the item to the security office OR post it here immediately. Do not keep found items for more than 24 hours. Update the status to "Claimed" once the owner collects it.
⚠️ Claiming an Item To claim an item, contact the person who reported it via the contact information listed. The security office may ask you to provide proof of ownership before releasing any item.
